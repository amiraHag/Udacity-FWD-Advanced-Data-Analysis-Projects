Introduction¶

In this project we will analyze data of 10,000 movies collected from The Movie Database (TMDb), including user ratings, revenue, budget and many more data related to each movie

In Particular we will address the following questions:

  • what is the 5 most popular movies?
  • What is the 5 most costable movies ?
  • What is the 5 most revenue movies ?
  • What is the heighest 5 vote count movies ?
  • What is the heighest 5 vote average movies ?
  • Is there a relation between budget and revenue?
  • Is there a relation between popularity and budget?
  • Is there a relation between popularity and revenue?
  • what is the top 10 years related to movie released count?
  • What is the top 10 years related to popularity?
  • What is the top 10 years related to budget?
  • What is the top 10 years related to revenue?
  • What is the most frequent genres?
  • What is the most popular genres?
  • What is the most costable genres?
  • What is the most profitable genres?
  • Who is the Cast Member that participated in highest no of movies?
  • What is The top 5 Production companies with highest money spent in budget?
